Indoor Competition Standards

Indoor volleyballs are built for speed and tactile feedback. They are designed for use on sprung timber floors and must be kept away from moisture.

Technical Specifications

  • Model: Mikasa V200W (FIVB Official).
  • Surface: Double-dimple microfiber technology.
  • Weight: 260-280 grams.
  • Air Pressure: 0.30 to 0.325 kgf/cm².
