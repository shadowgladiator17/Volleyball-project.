Detailed History of the Sport

Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan in Holyoke, Massachusetts. Originally called Mintonette, it was created as an indoor sport. By 1896, the name changed to Volleyball. The first custom ball was made by Spalding in 1900. Since then, the ball has evolved from leather panels to the heat-bonded synthetic shells used in 2026.
